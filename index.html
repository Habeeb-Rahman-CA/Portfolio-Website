A Glimpse into My Journey
I'm Habeeb Rahman, a passionate and dedicated MERN stack developer. I hold a degree in BCom Computer Application. My fascination with technology and problem-solving led me to explore coding during my studies. As I delved into programming, I discovered my enthusiasm for web development, which quickly became my true calling. I am now focused on building and creating innovative web applications.

During my studies, I realized that accounting was not the right path for me as I found it uninteresting. During my vacation, I decided to self-learn some basic coding, and it turned out to be a great fit. This discovery led me to pursue web development, and I have never looked back since. I am now confident that I am on the right path and continually strive to improve my skills.

My journey into web development began with hands-on experience in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript. I am continually expanding my knowledge in modern web technologies. Recently, I completed several projects, including a music website and a site called Presento, showcasing my ability to build functional and visually appealing web applications.

In addition to my technical skills, I am continuously learning and exploring new frameworks and tools. I believe in clean, efficient code and am always looking for opportunities to improve and innovate.
